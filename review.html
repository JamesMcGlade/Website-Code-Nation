Describe ways to improve productivity and efciency

This is what I have gathered from previous working experience and from this whole project regarding being more productive and efficient

  • For any queries I may have regarding CSS or HTML I will always consult them on W3Schools. This site had almost every answer for my bumps along the road.
  • Watch tutorials on Youtube. Watching the whole process of building a webpage can be really handy when coming up with ideas or to make clear a part of the coding process.
  • Don't be reluctant to ask questions. I know for a fact that if it wasn't for the help of my peers and our instructors Sam and Leona I would have spent much more time trying to figure out how to solve whatever inconvenient I faced.
  • Take short breaks from time to time. Being in front of a screen all day is not only tiring but also could inflict stress. Having a few minutes to strech your muscles or rest for a bit will increase your performance.
